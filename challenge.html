Results

After Completing the 5 Day Wellness that Works Challenge, You Will:

  • Be empowered to bravely and vulnerably show up for yourself and your career.
  • Have the confidence to align choices with your goals, even on tour.
  • Learn a repeatable framework to close the curtain on EVERY goal

Topics Covered in The Wellness that Works Challenge Include:

  • Reframing wellness beliefs around diet culture
  • Goal setting and achieving
  • Breaking down lofty goals into small, manageable action steps
  • Prioritizing responsibilities to streamline energy

What is Included?

  • Access to our Private Pop-Up Facebook Group for our Ensemble to Communicate
  • Live 30 Minute Lessons with Guidance Designed for Your Unique Needs
  • A Bonus Live Group Q&A to get immediate feedback and clarification
  • PDF resources, overviews, and checklists to guide you every step of the way
  • Our community, the Ensemble, is a supportive, engaging community perfect for support and motivation
  • Accountability check-ins to make sure you don't fall behind

Who is The Wellness that Works Challenge for?

This Free 5 Day Challenge is Exclusively for Singer/Songwriters, Musicians, Actors, Dancers, Circus Artists, Pole and Burlesque Performers, and Other Performing Artists, Like You, Who Want:

  • To learn to take a goal from a dream to action
  • To feel actions are aligned with goals
  • To feel confident making decisions, even during tour and rehearsal
  • To learn to prioritize and achieve goals
  • A framework to use to reach every goal
