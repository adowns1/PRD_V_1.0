Results

After Completing the 12 Week Fuel Your Fame Program, You Will:

  • Have the Energy to be Fully Booked and Rock Out Night After Night
  • Have the Confidence to Plan Your Meals to Align with Your Goals, Even on Tour.
  • Learn a Repeatable Framework for Smashing Every Goal

Topics covered in Fuel Your Fame include:

  • Goal Setting and Planning
  • Strategies and Tactics for Improving Wellness
  • Intuitive Eating
  • Fueling for Off-Season and Auditions
  • Fueling Rehearsals and Tech
